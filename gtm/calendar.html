ONE JOB: TAKE A SEAT AT
THE FRAMEWORK TABLE.

For 22 days we earn ALB’DO a place in the web-framework conversation — not as another tool, as a different idea of what a framework is: you write the frontend, and the backend is a tier the compiler emits. Every post is about the product. No origin story, no “our journey” — the work speaks. We reveal the idea and the craft. We hold the numbers, and we hold two stages, for 15 August.

Consumers

The builders shipping on Next / Astro / Remix today. Pulled by the heresy and the pain of the stack they maintain by hand.

“this deletes my backend folder”

Early adopters

The first hands who chase new frameworks for sport. Pulled by craft, determinism, and being first through the door.

“i want to build on this now”

Investors

Pulled by a credible new entry in a huge, contested category — and by proof (durability, DX) shown, not claimed.

“new paradigm, real demo”

IN PLAY NOW

ALB’DO — the frontend framework (Albedo · the mirror).
FORGE — the backend the compiler emits (Nigredo · the crucible).
PHOSPHOR — the runtime glow, used only as light/aesthetic.

HELD — AFTER 15 AUG

CTRNI’TAS (AI) and RUB’DO (fintech) stay dark. Do not name, tease with detail, or use their stage-colours (gold / oxblood) before the reveal. At the reveal they appear only as two locked stages.

THE GOVERNING PARADOX

Generate hype without hype language. The loudest thing in the feed is the quietest voice in the room. Every rival shouts “game-changer”; we speak like a lab logbook and a poet at once. Cover the handle — the post still reads dark · precious · transmuted · slightly alive. If it reads generic SaaS, we don’t post it.

THE PRODUCTION SPEC.

The whole campaign is one look. 90% monochrome, 10% stage-colour, aberration only at light edges. If a comp looks colourful, it is wrong.

PALETTE — IN USE

Forge Black#08090Bthe ground
Argent#C9CACEchrome · hero
Glass#BFE9F2refraction · clarity
Phosphor#B8FFE3the bloom · rim-light
Forge Ember#FF7A2Fheat, inside FORGE only
Aberrationcyan/magsalt · at edges only
Citrinitas#E8B33DHELD · do not use
Rubedo#C1272DHELD · do not use

Materials

Chrome (hero, mirror-polished, banded, always flowing) · Glass (secondary, refraction) · Void (near-black ground) · Bloom (light as material, a white rim behind every object).

Type

Display — heavy grotesque, tight, uppercase, negative tracking (Neue Haas / Archivo Black weight). Marginalia — monospace, small, wide-tracked: dates, coordinates, spec captions, barcodes.

The apostrophe

ALB’DO — the hero glyph. A chrome sliver / a bead of mercury with a cyan-magenta seam. Never generic punctuation. Doubles as the favicon seed.

Composition

One object, centred, floating in black with a bloom behind it. Spec-sheet scaffolding — hairlines, corner ticks, rotated labels, mono coordinates, index numbers. A catalogue plate of a rare artifact. The luxury is the emptiness.

DELIVERY FORMATS
